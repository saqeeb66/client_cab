Terms & Conditions

A simple, transparent structure to keep your trips predictable and stress-free.

Usage & Billing

  • Kms and Hrs are calculated garage to garage for all Local, Disposal and Outstation trips.
  • Minimum billing for Outstation trips is based on 300 Km per day per calendar day.
  • Chauffeur retention charges apply for Day / Night halts as per the published rate card.
  • Toll, parking, interstate permit fees and any other statutory charges are to be borne by the customer.

Bookings, Changes & Cancellations

  • All bookings are subject to vehicle availability at the time of confirmation.
  • Last-minute changes to itinerary, pickup time or vehicle type may impact pricing.
  • Cancellations close to the pickup time may attract cancellation charges depending on dispatch status.

Passenger Responsibilities

  • Wearing seat belts is recommended for all occupants for safety.
  • Any damage to vehicle interiors or accessories caused during the trip will be billed to the customer.
  • Transport of illegal items, hazardous materials or banned substances is strictly prohibited.

Privacy Policy

How Arcot Cabs uses and protects your information when you travel with us.

Arcot Cabs respects your privacy. We collect only the information necessary to process your bookings and deliver a safe travel experience.

Information We Collect

  • Your name, contact number, email address and pickup / drop locations when you make an enquiry or booking.
  • Trip-related information such as date, time, distance and vehicle type to manage billing and service quality.

How We Use Your Information
